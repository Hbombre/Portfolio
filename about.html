About Me

I live in Sylvan Lake, AB with my wife and three kids. I have always had a passion for computers and write code every day. I attended a Computer Information Systems program in 2009 and released my first app on the app store in 2012.


I love the challenge of creating software. It's like building a jigsaw puzzle. You have a vision of what it's going to look like in the end, but the fun is putting it together.
